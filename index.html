What is 3p0ch?

3p0ch (pronounced "Epoch") is the first hobbyist-level AI-autonomous combat robot that learns from its losses.

Built with an onboard NVIDIA Jetson Orin Nano, 3p0ch captures training data during matches and stores it in MinIO AIStor for AI model refinement.

Every fight is a new epoch. Every loss is a learning opportunity. The robot improves with each iteration.

The robot learns from its losses.
